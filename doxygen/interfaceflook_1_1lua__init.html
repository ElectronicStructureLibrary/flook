Detailed Description

Creates new LUA environments by starting LUA.

If the luaState is an already opened LUA environment, it will be closed by an call lua_close(luaState).

Then, a new LUA environment will be created and will be made available to interact with LUA.

You cannot interact with LUA until you have called lua_init(luaState).
